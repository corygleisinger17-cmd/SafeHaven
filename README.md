# SafeHaven

> **Everything important about your home. All in one place.**

---

## Overview

SafeHaven is an AI-powered household operating system that helps homeowners organize, maintain, and protect everything they own.

Rather than storing information across filing cabinets, email inboxes, cloud folders, and memory, SafeHaven creates a single trusted digital home for household knowledge.

SafeHaven combines inventory management, receipt scanning, warranty tracking, maintenance reminders, AI-powered search, and intelligent recommendations into one seamless experience.

---

## Mission

Reduce the mental burden of homeownership by creating the world's most trusted household intelligence platform.

---

## Vision

To become the operating system for homeownership.

SafeHaven will provide homeowners with a secure, intelligent, and organized digital memory of everything they own while proactively helping them maintain, protect, and improve their homes.

---

## Core Features

### Household Setup Wizard

Guided onboarding that walks homeowners through their house room by room.

---

### Inventory Management

Store everything important about household assets.

- Appliances
- Electronics
- Furniture
- Tools
- Outdoor equipment
- HVAC
- Vehicles (future)

---

### AI Document Processing

Upload:

- Receipts
- Manuals
- Warranties
- Invoices

SafeHaven automatically extracts useful information.

---

### Warranty Tracking

Track warranty status and receive proactive reminders before expiration.

---

### Maintenance Management

Never forget routine household maintenance again.

Examples:

- HVAC filters
- Smoke detectors
- Water softener
- Furnace service
- Deck sealing

---

### Natural Language Search

Ask questions naturally.

Examples:

> Where is my dishwasher manual?

> What size furnace filter do I need?

> Which warranties expire this year?

---

### AI Household Insights

SafeHaven identifies:

- Missing documentation
- Upcoming maintenance
- Warranty opportunities
- Household trends
- Incomplete records
