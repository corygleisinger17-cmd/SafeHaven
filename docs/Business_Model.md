# Purpose

This document defines SafeHaven's business strategy, target market, pricing model, revenue model, and long-term growth strategy.

---

# Business Summary

SafeHaven is an AI-powered SaaS platform that helps homeowners organize, maintain, and manage everything they own.

SafeHaven operates on a recurring subscription model while continuously expanding customer value through intelligent household management.

---

# Target Market

## Primary Customers

Homeowners

Characteristics:

- Ages 30–65
- Own one or more homes
- Comfortable using smartphones
- Value organization
- Want to simplify homeownership

---

## Secondary Customers

- New homeowners
- Families
- DIY enthusiasts
- Busy professionals

---

## Future Markets

- Landlords
- Property managers
- Vacation property owners
- Small businesses

# Customer Problems

SafeHaven solves problems such as:
